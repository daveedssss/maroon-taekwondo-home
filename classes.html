Training Programs

At Dave Hermino Taekwondo, we offer a variety of programs designed to meet the needs of students at every level. From beginners just starting their martial arts journey to experienced practitioners looking to refine their skills, we have a class that's right for you.

Children's Program (Ages 4-12)

Schedule: Mon, Wed, Fri - 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM

Our children's program focuses on developing fundamental motor skills, discipline, and confidence in a fun, safe environment. Children learn Taekwondo basics while building character and respect.

Enroll Now

Teen Program (Ages 13-17)

Schedule: Mon, Wed, Fri - 5:30 PM to 6:30 PM

Designed specifically for teenagers, this program builds upon basic techniques while addressing the unique physical and emotional development of this age group. Focus on discipline, respect, and self-confidence.

Enroll Now

Adult Program (Ages 18+)

Schedule: Tue, Thu - 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM, Sat - 9:00 AM to 10:30 AM

Our adult program offers a comprehensive approach to Taekwondo training, focusing on physical fitness, stress relief, and self-defense skills. Perfect for beginners and experienced practitioners alike.

Enroll Now

Competition Team

Schedule: By invitation - Sat 11:00 AM to 1:00 PM

For dedicated students looking to compete at local, regional, and national tournaments. Advanced training in sparring, forms, and breaking techniques. By invitation only after demonstrating commitment and skill.

Belt Progression System

Our belt system follows the traditional Taekwondo progression, allowing students to track their progress and set clear goals.

White Beginning the journey
Yellow Foundation of basics
Green Growth in skills
Blue Rising knowledge
Red Approaching mastery
Black Beginning of true learning
